Rating My Favorite Movies

Each of my favorite movies are unique and profound in their own way. Let's look at them objectively and critique them based on their overall quality! (Ratings are based on my own opinion)

Ice Princess

Ice Princess is truly a hidden gem in terms of Disney movies. It tells us the story of an intelligent bookworm who uses her love of physics and combines it with her love of figure skating. My only nitpick about this movie is how inaccurately they portrayed her skating progress. Although despite this, it still deserves 5 stars for being a classic uplifting romcom.

Star Rating

Rio 2

Rio 2 is simply an amazing and adventure-packed movie. In contrary to the first movie, Blu finally becomes much more confident in himself now that he is surrounded by his new friends and children. The reason why I love this movie more than its first part (even though I throughly enjoyed Rio) is because of how it makes the forest feel incredibly lively and beautiful.

Star Rating

The Bee Movie

The Bee Movie is so incredibly satire and hilarious to the point that it comes around as genius. The fact that the rest of the humans take the bees extremely seriously and treating them as if they were humans themselves is completely something unorthodox and unexpected. The cherry on top of this "joke-taken-seriously" is the romance between Vanessa, a human and Barry, a bee.
